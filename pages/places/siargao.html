About

Why visit Siargao?

Known as the Surfing Capital of the Philippines, Siargao offers much more than perfect waves. Cloud 9 is world-famous for its barreling surf, but beyond that, the island is filled with hidden lagoons, palm-lined roads, and emerald rock pools. Siargao’s laid-back vibe attracts surfers, backpackers, and nature lovers seeking an authentic island escape.

Highlights

• Surfing at Cloud 9 • Sugba Lagoon and Magpupungko Rock Pools • Island hopping to Daku, Guyam, and Naked Islands • Motorbike rides through palm forests • Yoga and sunset chill spots

Good to know

The dry season (March to October) is perfect for surfing and exploring. Wi-Fi can be spotty — unplug and embrace the island pace. Always respect local surf etiquette and keep the beaches plastic-free.
