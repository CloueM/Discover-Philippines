About

Why visit Palawan?

Palawan is often called the “Last Frontier” of the Philippines, home to stunning limestone cliffs, hidden lagoons, and crystal-clear waters. El Nido and Coron offer breathtaking island-hopping adventures, while the Puerto Princesa Underground River — a UNESCO World Heritage Site — showcases nature’s wonders. The island’s natural beauty and rich marine life make it a paradise for divers and eco-travelers alike.

Highlights

• El Nido’s Big Lagoon and Secret Beach • Coron’s Kayangan Lake and shipwreck diving • Puerto Princesa Underground River • Snorkeling with sea turtles • Pristine beaches and untouched islands

Good to know

The dry season (November to May) is ideal for exploring Palawan. Many destinations are accessible by boat only, so prepare for island-hopping days. Bring eco-friendly sunscreen and respect marine sanctuaries.
