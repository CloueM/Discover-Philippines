Essentials

Broth of Warmth

The base is a clear beef broth created by boiling the shanks for several hours. Seasoned with peppercorns, fish sauce, and a pinch of salt, it’s a soothing, savory soup that captures the heart of Filipino comfort cooking.

Protein

The highlight of bulalo is its beef shank, complete with bone marrow. The long, slow simmering releases the marrow’s buttery flavor, enriching the broth and creating a comforting, meaty aroma that defines the dish.

Veggies

Bulalo’s vegetables bring balance and freshness. Corn on the cob adds sweetness, while cabbage, pechay (bok choy), and green beans bring crisp texture. Onions and garlic are simmered along with the meat to enhance the broth’s depth.
